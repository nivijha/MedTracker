### MedTracker

**MedTracker** is a full-stack **healthcare management system** initially focused on empowering **patients** to **securely store, organize, and access their medical records** in one place. The platform helps users track medication schedules, manage appointments, and monitor their overall health through an intuitive dashboard.

In the next phase, MedTracker will expand to include hospital-side features, allowing admins to manage patient data, prescriptions, and reports efficiently.
Looking ahead, the system aims to integrate **AI-powered** insights that can provide **personalized health suggestions** — such as reminders for missed doses, potential medicine interactions, or lifestyle recommendations — making **healthcare smarter and more proactive**.
